# Caribbean Business Site Checklist

Free guide from Buzprout Academy. Use this before you build (or before you hire us).

## Before you build

- [ ] I know who the site is for (one clear customer type)
- [ ] I know the one action I want visitors to take (call, book, WhatsApp, buy)
- [ ] I have a business name, phone, and email that match my real operations
- [ ] I checked if a simple Google Business Profile is enough for now

## Content

- [ ] Headline says what I do and who it is for (not just my business name)
- [ ] About section builds trust (who you are, why you, where you operate)
- [ ] Contact path matches how Caribbean customers actually reach you (often WhatsApp)
- [ ] Photos are real (your team, work, location), not only stock images
- [ ] Copy was edited after AI draft (sounds like you, not generic)

## Design and mobile

- [ ] Site readable on a phone without pinching or zooming
- [ ] Text contrast is strong enough outdoors on a phone screen
- [ ] Logo and colors look consistent on social profiles and the site
- [ ] Page loads in under a few seconds on mobile data

## Trust and findability

- [ ] Google Business Profile claimed and matches site details
- [ ] Basic SEO: page titles and descriptions mention location and service
- [ ] Testimonials, reviews, or client logos if you have them
- [ ] Privacy/contact info visible (email or WhatsApp, not a broken form)

## When DIY is enough

You probably only need a builder (Wix, Framer, etc.) if:

- You need 1 to 5 pages and rarely change content
- You do not need bookings, dashboards, or custom logic
- You are okay updating the site yourself

## When to level up

Talk to Buzprout (Flex or full build) if:

- You need bookings, payments, or admin tools
- WhatsApp and spreadsheets are breaking as you grow
- You need delivery tracking, client portals, or multi-user systems
- DIY site gets traffic but not enquiries
