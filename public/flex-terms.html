Buzprout Flex Terms

Last updated: July 2026

This is a plain-language summary of Buzprout Flex. Your signed Flex Schedule and Project Quote are the binding agreement.

What Flex is

Flex lets you pay a deposit upfront and spread the remaining build cost over 6 to 18 months with a plan fee on the financed portion. It is a commercial payment plan between you and Buzprout, not a loan from a bank.

Payment structure

  1. You pay the deposit on signing.
  2. Work begins after the deposit clears.
  3. You pay equal monthly installments for the agreed term.
  4. Plan fee rates: 6 months 10%; 12 months 15%; 18 months 20% of the financed portion (not the deposit).
  5. Late payments follow our Payment Policy.

Ownership during Flex

  • During the Flex term: Buzprout retains ownership of deliverables, source code, and hosting accounts (where we register on your behalf).
  • License: You may operate the deliverables for your business while payments are current.
  • Transfer: After final payment, we assign ownership and hand over repo, hosting, and domain per the agreed checklist.
  • Default: If you miss two consecutive installments or fail to cure within 30 days, we may pause the service, restrict admin access, and accelerate the remaining balance.

Scope changes

Changes outside the signed scope are quoted separately and do not extend the Flex term unless both parties agree in writing.

Early payoff

You may pay the remaining balance early. A 5% discount on the remaining balance may apply if paid in full before month 6 when offered in your Project Quote.

Personal guarantee

For financed portions over JMD 500,000, Buzprout may require a personal guarantee from the business principal.
