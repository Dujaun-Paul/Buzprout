Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This site uses cookies and similar technologies. See also our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites work, remember preferences, or understand usage.

Cookies we use

  • Essential: Required for basic site operation and security.
  • Analytics (if enabled): Anonymous usage data to improve the site (pages visited, browser type).
  • Third-party scheduling: When you book via Calendly, Calendly may set its own cookies under its policy.

Third-party services

Links and embeds may set cookies from:

  • Calendly (scheduling)
  • WhatsApp (when you open a chat link)
  • Hosting and CDN providers

We do not sell your data. Third parties process data under their own privacy policies.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect how the site works.
