# Buzprout Flex — Pilot Checklist

Use this before and during the first 2–3 Flex clients. Goal: validate terms, collections, and upsell to Care without burning margin.

## Before first Flex client

- [ ] Attorney reviewed Flex Schedule and Payment Policy drafts
- [ ] Internal calculator used; deal passes margin checklist
- [ ] Eligibility scorecard completed for client
- [ ] Project Quote states: Cash Price, Deposit, Term, Plan Fee, Monthly amount, Total collected
- [ ] Scope document signed and fixed
- [ ] Hosting and domain on Buzprout-controlled accounts
- [ ] Payment method committed (auto-pay or calendar reminders set)

## Client profile (pilot targets)

Prefer:
- Growth tier need (bookings, leads, light systems)
- Can meet 40%+ deposit
- Business or sole trader with verifiable revenue
- Caribbean-based, WhatsApp-responsive

Avoid for pilot:
- First-time client with no references and max term request
- Scope creep personality
- Demands full ownership before final payment

## During pilot

- [ ] Invoice on schedule (Day 0)
- [ ] Log all client contact re payments
- [ ] Test pause procedure on staging before needing it in production
- [ ] At 50% paid: discuss Care Essential or Standard post-transfer
- [ ] At 100% paid: execute IP handover checklist within 5 business days

## After pilot (2–3 clients)

- [ ] Record: average days to pay, any late payments, actual admin time
- [ ] Adjust Plan Fee or deposit % if margin below target
- [ ] Publish Flex section on website if legal cleared
- [ ] Train discovery calls: cash price first, then Flex quote

## Success metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| On-time payment rate | 90%+ |
| Pilot clients completing term | 2 of 3 |
| Post-handover Care conversion | 50%+ |
| No unpaid write-offs in pilot | Yes |

## Handover checklist (final payment)

- [ ] Signed IP assignment
- [ ] Repo transferred to client or client org
- [ ] Domain transferred or client registrar access
- [ ] Hosting migrated or credentials delivered
- [ ] Care plan offered (Essential or Standard)
