# Buzprout — Sales Decision Tree

Use on discovery calls. Follow the **business framework first**, then quote **cash price** and match the payment path.

## Step 1 — Identify the business outcome

Ask: *"What do you want to improve?"*

Listen for:

- More bookings / enquiries
- Fewer support calls / WhatsApp messages
- Better customer experience
- Less manual work / operational visibility
- Ability to scale

If they only describe a deliverable ("I need a website"), redirect: *"What problem would that solve for the business?"*

---

## Step 2 — Quantify the cost

Ask:

- How much time is being lost per week?
- How many enquiries or bookings are missed?
- How much revenue is left on the table?
- What breaks when volume increases?

Write down before/after metrics for the quote.

---

## Step 3 — Design the technology (scope)

Map outcome → solution tier:

| Outcome signal | Likely tier |
|----------------|-------------|
| Credibility, found online | Entry (Visibility) |
| Bookings, leads, conversion | Growth (Conversion) |
| Dashboards, tracking, internal tools | Systems (Operations) |
| Ongoing improvements | Retainers |

Confirm scope in outcome language on the quote — not a feature laundry list.

---

## Step 4 — Measure success

Agree success criteria before pricing:

- Before: ___ hours/week on messages
- After target: ___ hours/week
- Before: ___ bookings/week
- After target: ___ bookings/week

Include in quote under **Success metrics**.

---

## Payment path (after cash price is set)

### 1. Can they pay 40%+ now and the rest at milestones?

**→ Milestone build** (default, best margin)

Example: JMD 400,000 → 40% signing, 30% design approval, 30% launch.

### 2. Need it live but can pay monthly?

**→ Buzprout Flex** (12 months, 15% plan fee on financed portion)

Example: JMD 400,000 → JMD 160,000 deposit + JMD 23,000/mo × 12.

See [`flex-pricing-calculator.csv`](../internal/flex-pricing-calculator.csv).

### 3. Can't meet Flex deposit?

**→ Build + Care** (entry tier only) **OR walk away**

Example: JMD 120,000 site → JMD 30,000 deposit + JMD 7,500/mo × 18.

### 4. Already running custom software?

**→ Retainer only** (no new Flex debt)

Care Essential / Standard for sites; Systems retainer for apps.

### 5. Only need a basic presence / too expensive?

**→ Buzprout Academy** (DIY course) OR honest referral to a builder

Graduates get JMD 20,000 off first Flex deposit.

## Objection quick refs

| They say | You say |
|----------|---------|
| We just need a website | What business problem should the site solve? More bookings, credibility, or something else? |
| Why not Wix? | Module 4 in Academy, or Flex when you outgrow templates |
| Why not ChatGPT? | Course teaches AI properly; we build production systems |
| JMD 15K maintenance? | Essential from JMD 5K (hosting only); Standard adds 2 hrs edits |
| Too expensive upfront | Flex or Build + Care monthly path |
| How do I know it will work? | We agree before/after metrics upfront — hours saved, bookings, calls reduced |

---

## Guiding question

Before closing: **How does this improve the client's business — and can we measure it?**

If the answer is unclear, narrow scope or walk away.
