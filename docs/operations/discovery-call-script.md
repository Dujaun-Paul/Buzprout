# Buzprout — Discovery Call Script

30-minute call. No hard sell. Outcome-first.

---

## Opening (2 min)

> "Thanks for booking. This call is about understanding your business — not pitching you a website. I'll ask what's not working, what success looks like, and whether we're the right fit. Sound good?"

---

## Step 1 — Identify the business outcome (8 min)

**Primary question:**

> "What business problem are you trying to solve right now?"

**Follow-ups if they describe a deliverable:**

> "If we fixed that, what would change for the business — more bookings, fewer calls, less manual work?"

**Listen for outcome category:**

- Acquire customers
- Improve communication
- Improve customer experience
- Streamline operations
- Scale the business

**Stage check (optional):**

> "Are you mainly establishing credibility online, growing into bookings and automation, or scaling custom systems?"

---

## Step 2 — Quantify the cost (8 min)

> "Help me understand what this is costing you today."

**Prompts:**

- How many hours per week does your team spend on [repetitive messages / manual tracking / status updates]?
- How many enquiries or bookings do you think you miss because [no online booking / slow response / no visibility]?
- What breaks when you get busier?
- Have you tried fixing this before? What happened?

**Write down before metrics** — you'll use these on the quote.

---

## Step 3 — Design the technology (8 min)

> "Based on what you've described, here's what I'd recommend — and why."

**Frame as:** Problem → Impact → Solution → Outcome

**Examples:**

- *"Customers can't book after hours → missed revenue → online booking system → bookings while you sleep."*
- *"Orders lost in WhatsApp → chaos as you grow → dispatch dashboard → one calm view of every job."*

**Scope boundaries:**

- What's in v1 vs later
- What they already use (CRM, WhatsApp, payments)
- Realistic timeline

**If budget is unclear:**

> "Our entry packages start around JMD 60K for visibility. Growth builds with booking and systems run JMD 250K+. I'll send a firm quote after we confirm scope — does that range work as a starting point?"

---

## Step 4 — Measure success (2 min)

> "If we work together, how would you know it worked?"

**Agree 1–3 metrics:**

- Hours saved per week
- Bookings or enquiries per week
- Support messages or calls per day
- Conversion rate, response time, etc.

> "I'll put these on the quote as before/after targets so we're aligned on what success means."

---

## Payment path (2 min)

Quote **cash price first**, then:

> "You can pay milestones — deposit, design approval, launch — or spread it with Buzprout Flex if monthly works better."

See [sales-decision-tree.md](./sales-decision-tree.md) for branches.

---

## Close

**Good fit:**

> "I'll send a quote with the outcome, metrics, scope, and payment options within [X days]. Any questions before we wrap?"

**Not a fit / too early:**

> "Honestly, [Academy / a simple builder / waiting until X] might be the better move right now. I'd rather tell you that than sell you something you don't need."

**Academy redirect:**

> "If you're not ready for a custom build, our Academy walks you through launching yourself. Graduates get JMD 20K off their first Flex deposit when they're ready to level up."

---

## Objection language (brand voice)

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We just need a website" | "Totally — what should that site do for the business? Get enquiries, take bookings, build trust?" |
| "Too expensive" | "What would fixing this be worth per month in time or revenue saved? Let's see if Flex or a smaller first phase makes sense." |
| "Can ChatGPT do this?" | "AI is great for drafts. Production systems — bookings, payments, automation — need proper setup. That's what we build." |
| "Why not Wix?" | "For a simple brochure site, sometimes Wix is enough. When you need bookings, dashboards, or integrations, that's where we come in." |
| "How long?" | "Websites: 3–6 weeks. Custom software: 6–16 weeks. We'll confirm on the quote after scope." |
