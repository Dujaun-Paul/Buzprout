# Buzprout Flex Schedule (Draft for Legal Review)

**Status:** Draft only. Not legal advice. Have a Jamaica-qualified attorney review before use.

**Attach to:** Master Services Agreement (MSA)

---

## 1. Purpose

This Flex Schedule sets payment terms when Client chooses **Buzprout Flex** instead of full upfront or milestone payment for a defined Scope of Work.

## 2. Definitions

- **Cash Price:** The price Client would pay if the Scope were paid in full before work begins.
- **Deposit:** Non-refundable amount due before work starts, as stated in the Project Quote.
- **Financed Portion:** Cash Price minus Deposit.
- **Plan Fee:** Extended payment fee applied to the Financed Portion (not the Deposit).
- **Flex Term:** Number of months over which the Financed Portion plus Plan Fee is paid.

## 3. Payment structure

1. Client pays the Deposit on signing.
2. Buzprout begins work after Deposit clears.
3. Client pays equal monthly installments for the Flex Term.
4. Plan Fee rates: 6 months 10%; 12 months 15%; 18 months 20% of Financed Portion.
5. Late payments: 7-day grace, then late fee as stated in the Project Quote.

## 4. Intellectual property and ownership

1. **During Flex Term:** Buzprout retains ownership of all deliverables, source code, hosting accounts, and domain registration (where Buzprout registers on Client's behalf).
2. **License:** Client receives a non-exclusive license to operate the deliverables for Client's business while payments are current.
3. **Transfer:** On receipt of final payment, Buzprout assigns ownership to Client via signed IP assignment and transfers repo, hosting, and domain per agreed handover checklist.
4. **Default:** If Client misses two consecutive installments or fails to cure within 30 days, Buzprout may pause the live service, restrict admin access, and declare the remaining balance immediately due (acceleration).

## 5. Scope changes

Changes outside the signed Scope are quoted separately and do not extend the Flex Term unless both parties agree in writing.

## 6. Early payoff

Client may pay the remaining balance early. Optional 5% discount on remaining balance if paid in full before month 6 (if offered in Project Quote).

## 7. Personal guarantee

For Financed Portion over JMD 500,000, Buzprout may require a personal guarantee from Client's principal.
