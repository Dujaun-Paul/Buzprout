## 1. Parties

**Buzprout** ("Provider")  
**Client** ("Client")

## 2. Services

Provider will perform digital design, development, and related services as described in each signed Project Quote, Statement of Work, or Flex Schedule.

## 3. Fees and payment

3.1 Client pays fees stated in the Project Quote.  
3.2 Invoices are due within seven (7) days unless otherwise stated.  
3.3 Late payments may incur fees and service suspension as described in the Payment Policy.  
3.4 Deposits are non-refundable after scope lock except as required by law.

## 4. Intellectual property

4.1 **Cash / milestone projects:** Upon receipt of full payment, Provider assigns deliverables to Client subject to third-party licenses.  
4.2 **Flex / Build + Care:** Provider retains ownership until full payment; Client receives a license to operate deliverables while payments are current. Transfer on completion per Flex Schedule or Project Quote.  
4.3 Provider may reuse general know-how, libraries, and non-client-specific patterns.

## 5. Client responsibilities

Client will provide timely content, feedback, approvals, and access required to perform the work. Delays may extend timelines.

## 6. Change requests

Work outside signed scope is quoted separately.

## 7. Confidentiality

Each party will protect the other's confidential business information.

## 8. Limitation of liability

Provider's total liability is limited to fees paid for the project giving rise to the claim, except where law prohibits. Provider is not liable for indirect or consequential damages.

## 9. Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach if uncured within fourteen (14) days. Fees for work performed remain payable. Flex acceleration terms apply where signed.
